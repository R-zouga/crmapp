About the CRM Project

This End of Year Project represents a personal attempt to create a dedicated Customer Relationship Management System for small businesses and companies.
Sure, there are WAY BETTER SOLUTIONS than mine, but I honestly enjoyed the multiple challenges and complexities when creating this project and deploying it on the web. So for anyone who made it till this far and wants to use this project for personal or professional usage, PLEASE IT WILL MAKE ME PLEASED. Since the approach for this CRM was very generic and not oriented for specific usage, you can still tweak it to make it useful, for example:

  • You can make it for a car rental agency to keep track of its renting process and demand, get monthly reports for income and manage the car's availability and condition. All you have to do is add all necessary code to the Service app. Please refer to the Projects repo for further information.
  • You can make it for a consulting agency to keep track of its multiple services and client requests.
  • Same idea for small booking agencies, IT companies, and much, much more!

In conclusion, this project could be really interesting for anyone who really wants to take his Django skills to the next level, and why not even contribute to it. Best of luck and Thank You :)
